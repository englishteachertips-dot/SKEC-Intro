Course Overview

The SKEC course is structured around ten focused modules, each designed to give you practical, classroom-ready strategies and deep subject knowledge. From narrative writing through to behaviour for learning, you'll build a coherent teaching philosophy grounded in the latest research and proven techniques.

Each module includes video explanations, worked examples, and resources you can adapt for your own classes.

Resources & Downloads

All lesson plans, marking frameworks, vocabulary boxes, and teaching templates are available to download as you work through the course. Everything is formatted for easy use in Canva or your own design software.

You'll also find links to free ETCPD modules and additional reading recommendations tailored to each topic.
